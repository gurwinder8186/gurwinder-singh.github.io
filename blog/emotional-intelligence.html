Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence also known as Emotional Quotient (EQ) is the ability to understand, use and manage our own emotions in a positive way to battle stress, understand others around, overcome struggles and challenges in our life. It includes following three steps:

Practice of mindfulness- it is helpful in every aspect of life. Mindfulness aids us to better understand and relate to ourselves and the world. Its practice improves our attention and kindness for others and ourselves. We can make well choices and can be less sensitive with the introduction of mindfulness.

Self-control and knowledge: with attentive mind we can Analise the situation quicky and can react wisely. we can better understand our priorities, and who and how we are.

Creating useful mental habits: if we know our mind behave negatively in certain situations, we can develop a mental habit to tackle those situations in a positive way.

How is EQ different to IQ?

While IQ primarily measures cognitive abilities such as logical reasoning, problem-solving, and analytical skills, EQ refers to your aptitude for understanding and managing emotions, empathizing with others, and building effective interpersonal relationships.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is very important in life because it is at all times essential attribute, at work, socially and in personal life too. if you see the most successful people, they usually have way more EQ than IQ. IQ can help get the placement in the good institute but when the most important time comes such as exams it the EQ which relive stress and emotions to let the IQ to be used.
