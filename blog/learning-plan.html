Learning plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to work in the IT/software development industry, which offers a balanced work and personal life. I am passionate about coding and seek a career that provides flexibility, allowing me to enjoy hybrid work arrangements and spend quality time with my family.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths: I can spend extended periods on learning with patience and without rushing, which helps me grasp complex concepts thoroughly. Additionally, having moderate tech knowledge enhances my ability to understand and engage with technical material effectively.
Limitations: I sometimes struggle to maintain very focused study sessions due to being away from studies for long periods. Additionally, having English as a second language can present challenges, and I find it difficult to say no to additional commitments, which can affect my focus.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

While at Dev Academy, I aim to develop several key non-technical skills. I want to improve my efficiency in managing time and tasks more effectively. Enhancing my problem-solving approach will help me tackle challenges more effectively and develop sharper decision-making skills for better judgment and choices. Additionally, I seek to polish my interpersonal skills and communication abilities to interact and collaborate more effectively with others.

An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives

To work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators, and industry/community representatives, I will actively participate in all required tasks, be punctual, and respectful. I will also proactively ask for help when needed and offer assistance to others. Additionally, I will remain optimistic and open to receiving positive feedback from peers and facilitators, using it to make constructive changes and improve continuously.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

When things start building up and overwhelming me, I will dedicate more focus and engage in effective study sessions to address the challenges. Additionally, I will seek help through the proper channels if I need assistance with my research or any other difficulties.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will first utilize the available resources on the Dev Academy website. If I still need further assistance, I will research online and consult with fellow students. If the issue persists, I will post in the relevant group on Discord to seek help in a timely manner.
